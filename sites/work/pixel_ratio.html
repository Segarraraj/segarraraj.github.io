Pixel Ratio is a studio specialized in porting games to new platforms, ensuring high-quality experiences across mobile and console devices. Our team works on adapting existing titles to different hardware while preserving performance and gameplay integrity.

Foretales – Mobile Ports

Our first completed project at Pixel Ratio was the port of Foretales to iOS and Android using Unity. We handled technical challenges independently, reacting quickly to issues and delivering reliable solutions. This project strengthened our problem-solving skills and our ability to work autonomously while maintaining high-quality results.

Mindcop – Mobile (Android & iOS)

Currently, we are working on the mobile adaptation of Mindcop, originally a PC game. Our work involves platform-specific adjustments, performance optimization, and ensuring the gameplay experience translates smoothly to Android and iOS devices. This project highlights our adaptability and technical problem-solving capabilities in cross-platform development.

Tyrants Realm – Console (PS5 & Nintendo Switch)

Simultaneously, we are working on the console ports of Tyrants Realm, a PC game, adapting it for PS5 and Nintendo Switch. This involves performance optimization, platform-specific debugging, and ensuring a consistent gameplay experience. These tasks require careful technical planning and collaborative problem-solving, reinforcing our capabilities in complex cross-platform projects.
