That's the final project of my third year at ESAT, and it's the most ambitious and big project of my career. We formed a group of 7 programmers, 5 artists and 1 game designer to make a full game/demo. They gave us the genre (2.5D Platformer) and we envisioned that making a Metroidvania was a good idea.

This wasn't the first time we were working with artists, take a look at Temple of Tharsis, but indeed this time was the most serious attempt to make a full game. In this project, we really learned how to properly talk with other parts of the team that are not that technics as us while mastering our Unreal Engine skills.

We were also helped by two amazing musicians of Berklee College of Music that did an incredible job making all the effects, sounds and music of the game. That gave us the opportunity to learn a bit better how to plug sounds and music into our games.

The game is released in Steam as a Free To Play.
