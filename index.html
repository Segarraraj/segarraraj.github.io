My name is Jorge Segarra

I have been programing for the last 5 years.

I started studying at a professional training course, where I learned different languages and skills such as Java, SQL, Objective C or c#. After a year and a half studying there and three months as an intern in a small company, I got my first job where I worked as an intern.

During the year working at that company, I learned improved my previous skills and learned many new others. I worked mainly as a back-end and full-stack developer, but I also did small projects such as an Android APP to connect with their server.

Finally, I moved to ESAT where I'm currently. During my pass through ESAT I have learned new and interesting things, from learning C, C++ or Assembly to learning how 3D Renderers work and making our own game engine.
