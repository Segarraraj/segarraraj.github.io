My name is Jorge Segarra

I have been programing for the last 5 years.

I started studying at a professional training course, where I learned different languages and skills such as Java, SQL, Objective C or c#. After a year and a half studying there and three months as an intern in a small company, where I got my first job.

During the year working at that company, where I improved my previous skills and learned many new others. I worked mainly in the back-end, learning PHP and Laravel from scratch, but also working a bit in the front-end with vuejs that was new to me. I also did some small projects such as an Android APP.

Looking to improve, I started my studies at ESAT. During my pass through ESAT I have learned new and interesting things, from learning C, C++ or Assembly to learning how 3D Renderers work and making our own game engine.

To finalize my studies in the best way possible, I moved to Saxion in Enschede.
