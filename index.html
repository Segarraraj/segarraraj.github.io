My name is Jorge Segarra

A programmer who turns problems into solutions.

Hi, I’m Jorge Segarra, a programmer who enjoys solving technical problems and turning them into real, working solutions.

I’m a practical person — I like understanding what needs to be done, figuring out the best way to do it, and making it happen. I tend to be independent when tackling challenges, but I value teamwork and clear communication just as much.

I have a strong background in C++, a low-level language that taught me the fundamentals of programming and how systems really work under the hood. Right now, I work in the video game industry, focusing on console and mobile ports, where precision, adaptability, and understanding complex systems are essential — exactly the kind of environment where I thrive.
